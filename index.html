About Us

Restuca serves authentic Thai cuisine in the heart of Dhaka. Head Chef Pharatee Sakhunsong hails from Thailand and has a culinary career spanning 27 years, from hawker stalls in her homeland to restaurants in the United Kingdom. Her menu is based around the four main tastes of Thai cuisines – sweet, sour, spicy and salty – with ingredients flown in from Thailand. Regular patrons rave about the tom yam gung ya (king prawn soup) and the yam pak boong (white mushroom salad). Lovers of seafood will not be disappointed either with dishes like pla ka pong som rod (fried whole white snapper with sweet and sour sauce) and pla jaramed nung buoy (steamed pomfret with pickled plum) waiting to delight their palates.
